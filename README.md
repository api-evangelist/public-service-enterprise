# Public Service Enterprise Group (public-service-enterprise)

Public Service Enterprise Group (PSEG) is a Fortune 500 publicly traded diversified energy holding company headquartered in Newark, New Jersey. Through its subsidiaries including PSE&G and PSEG Power, PSEG provides regulated electric and natural gas distribution and operates electric power generation across the northeastern United States.
